mail (aerialis)

This container handles mail-related services and relays for the infrastructure, such as SMTP/IMAP relaying or filtering.

Nix expression

Configuration for the mail container on aerialis.

  config,
  lib,
  pkgs,
  sources,
  garuda-lib,
  sops,
  ...

    pname = "authres_status";
